## itch.io

**Title:** Carl's Doorway Dash
**Tagline:** A collapsing-dungeon game show you play with one thumb.
**Kind:** HTML (upload `index.html` + the `docs/` folder, or the whole repo; set `index.html` as the launch file). Embed size: 480 × 854, mobile-friendly.

**Description:**
> Carl is a barefoot contestant on a live dungeon game show with terrible ratings insurance. Outrun rolling logs, croc pits, swinging blades, tunnels and low branches across an ever-collapsing set of floors while the VIEWERS counter climbs and a snarky announcer narrates your poor decisions.
>
> **One thumb, tons of moves:** tap to jump, swipe to slide, double-tap to dive-kick a chaining ground slam, swipe up to dash-hop, and jump at vines to swing across. Chain near-misses for huge scores, bank gold into permanent perks, and tap like mad to outrun each floor's collapse.
>
> No installs, no accounts, no loading screens — it's a single HTML file with procedural pixel art and fully synthesized audio. Grab it and play in ten seconds.
>
> 🎮 Tips: don't just spam jump (low branches punish it), clear a floor to heal, and fire the DONUT MISSILE when the meter's full.

**Tags:** endless-runner, arcade, pixel-art, mobile, one-button, html5, singleplayer, high-score
